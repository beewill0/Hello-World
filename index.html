Reebok Questions



The history of "The Questions"




              • The Reebok Question was the first signature release of Professional Basketball Player Allen Iverson.
              • The first Reebok Question was released in 1996, the same year Allen Iverson was drafted into the NBA.
              • The inpiration behind the original shoe design was Micheal Jordans own shoe.
              • The Reebok Question would gain momemtum after Allen Iverson wore them in a showdown with MJ in which he used his killer crossover to shake the Jumpman.
              • This Reebok sneaker went on to be one of the best selling signature sneakers in Reebok history.
              • The Reebok Question Has come out with more the 100 different color ways.
              • In the year 2016 Reebok celebrated the 20th year anniversary of The Questions original release.
              • During the 20th Anniversary Reebok plans to release 20 different Questions with new and classic colorways.
